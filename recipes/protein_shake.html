My Personal Protein Shake

Chocolate-Banana Protein Shake

This personal chocolate-banana protein shake of mine is something that I've been having for almost two years now. I'm a fairly picky eater so I don't stick to things unless they taste good, so you know this tastes that good. It contains roughly 600 calories and a whopping 61 grams of protein!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 scoops of vanilla whey protein powder
  • 250ml 1% milk
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • a pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Cut your banana into smaller pieces, around 5 or 6.
  2. Place them into a blender. Your blender should be able to hold at least 600 ml!
  3. Add your milk to the blender
  4. Add your protein powder
  5. Add your cocoa powder, and gently shake to mix the powder well.
  6. Mix with the blender, shake as it mixes
  7. Pour it into a cup, and then you're done!
